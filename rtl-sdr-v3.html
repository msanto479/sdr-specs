RTL-SDR v3

Specifications based on RTL-SDR.com v4 datasheet, applied here for v3 reference.

Overview

The RTL-SDR v3 is one of the most widely used entry-level SDRs, based on the RTL2832U chipset. It is popular among hobbyists and beginners for its low cost, wide frequency coverage, and large community support. While it lacks transmit capabilities, it is well-suited for learning about SDR technology, monitoring signals, and experimenting with RF applications.
