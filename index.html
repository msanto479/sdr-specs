About SDR-Specs.com

This site is dedicated to cataloging and comparing the specifications of software-defined radios (SDRs) from across the industry. Whether you are a hobbyist, student, researcher, or professional, our goal is to provide a one-stop reference for SDR capabilities.

Use the navigation on the left to explore SDRs by category, learn terminology in the glossary, or check out additional SDR resources.
